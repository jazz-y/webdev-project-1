What Breed is Fluffy?

Fluffy is a mix between a maltese mother and a poodle father. Since Maltese dogs are small, the poodle parent is often a toy or miniature size. Maltipoo height and weight varies based on the parent poodle, often 8 to 14 inches tall and 5 to 20 pounds. Their coats can be scruffy, wavy, or curly with a white, silver, blue, grey, brown, black, cream, or come combination of colors.

Fur colors:

  • White
  • Silver
  • Blue
  • Grey
  • Brown
  • Black
  • Cream
  • Combination

Maltipoos are known for being "hypoallergenic," or rather having a lower possibility of an allergic reaction, due to their reduced amount of dander, lack of undercoat, and hair-like fur.

Maltipoo Behavior

Maltipoos are often very affectionate and loving towards their owners, and are prone to getting extremely attached to their owners, often becoming close with everyone in the family.
